About CPIHTC - Cumilla Polytechnic's High Tech Club

Founded in [YEAR], the Cumilla Polytechnic Institute High Tech Club (CPIHTC) is the premier technical and programming community for polytechnic students in Comilla, Bangladesh. We organize regular programming competitions, coding workshops, and tech seminars specifically designed for diploma engineering students at Cumilla Polytechnic.

Why Join CPIHTC at Cumilla Polytechnic?

  • Competitive Programming Contests: Monthly coding competitions following ICPC/IOI standards
  • Technical Workshops: Hands-on sessions on web development, algorithms, AI, and data structures
  • Industry Connections: Networking with tech companies and alumni from Comilla Polytechnic
  • Skill Development: Enhance your problem-solving, coding, and career readiness
  • Career Opportunities: Get noticed by recruiters through our coding contests and hackathons

How to Join CPIHTC at Cumilla Polytechnic

All current students of Cumilla Polytechnic Institute are eligible to join the High Tech Club:

  1. Attend our orientation session at the beginning of each semester
  2. Participate in at least one coding competition or workshop
  3. Register through our official CPIHTC portal
