About CPIHTC - Cumilla Polytechnic's High Tech Club

Founded in 2020, the Cumilla Polytechnic Institute High Tech Club (CPIHTC) is the premier technical and programming community for polytechnic students in Comilla, Bangladesh. We organize regular programming competitions, coding workshops, and tech seminars specifically designed for diploma engineering students at Cumilla Polytechnic.

Why Join CPIHTC at Cumilla Polytechnic?

  • Competitive Programming Contests: Monthly coding competitions following ICPC/IOI standards
  • Technical Workshops: Hands-on sessions on web development, algorithms, AI, and data structures
  • Industry Connections: Networking with tech companies and alumni from Comilla Polytechnic
  • Skill Development: Enhance your problem-solving, coding, and career readiness
  • Career Opportunities: Get noticed by recruiters through our coding contests and hackathons
  • ICPC Preparation: Specialized training for International Collegiate Programming Contest
  • Project Collaboration: Opportunities to work on real-world projects with fellow students

Upcoming Events at Cumilla Polytechnic Institute

CPIHTC hosts numerous tech events throughout the academic year at Comilla Polytechnic Institute campus:

Annual CPIHTC Programming Contest

The flagship event of Cumilla Polytechnic's High Tech Club, attracting participants from polytechnics across Bangladesh. This competitive programming contest tests algorithm knowledge, problem-solving skills, and coding efficiency.

Weekly Coding Challenges

Regular problem-solving sessions for CPI students to practice competitive programming and coding. These sessions help students prepare for national programming competitions and technical interviews.

Tech Talk Series

Invited speakers from Bangladesh's tech industry share insights with Comilla Polytechnic students. Past speakers have included software engineers from leading Bangladeshi tech companies and CPI alumni working in the technology sector.

Hackathon Events

Weekend hackathons where students collaborate to build projects solving real-world problems. These events encourage innovation, teamwork, and practical application of programming skills.

ICPC Training Program

Structured training program to prepare Cumilla Polytechnic students for the International Collegiate Programming Contest, covering advanced algorithms, data structures, and competitive programming strategies.

How to Join CPIHTC at Cumilla Polytechnic

All current students of Cumilla Polytechnic Institute are eligible to join the High Tech Club:

  1. Attend our orientation session at the beginning of each semester
  2. Participate in at least one coding competition or workshop
  3. Register through our official CPIHTC portal
  4. Join our online coding platform to start practicing

No prior programming experience is required to join - we offer beginner-friendly workshops and resources to help you get started with coding.
