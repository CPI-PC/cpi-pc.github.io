How to Join CPIPC at Cumilla Polytechnic

All current students of Cumilla Polytechnic Institute are eligible to join the programming club:

  1. Attend our orientation session at the beginning of each semester
  2. Participate in at least one coding competition or workshop
  3. Register through our official CPIPC portal
